Popular destinations in India

Mumbai

Massive city, home to bollywood, grand colonial buildings & bazaars with antiques and textiles.

Delhi

India's capital has Mughal-era Red Fort, Jama Masjid mosque & national monuments along the Rajpath.

Goa

State in India known for Arabian Sea beaches & European-style churches like Se Cathedral in Old Goa.

Jaipur

Known as "Pink City" for colored palaces & other 18th-century structures, including an observatory.

Bengaluru

India tech hub known for parks, nightlife, Bangalore Palace & Lalbagh Botanical Garden.

Chennai

City known for Tamil classical music & dance, plus Kapaleeshwarar Temple & colonial Fort St. George.

Udaipur

Rajasthan city known for lavish palaces, Jagdish Temple & artificial lakes, including Lake Pichola.

Agra

North Indian city with Mughal Empire landmarks like the Taj Mahal mausoleum & red-walled Agra Fort.

Kolkata

West Bengal capital home to Mother Teresa's tomb, British-colonial architecture & art galleries.

Varanasi

Sacred Ganges River city with some 2,000 Hindu temples (including Kashi Vishwanath) & Ramnagar Fort.

Shimla

Himachal Pradesh capital known for colonial architecture, Himalayan views & shopping on The Mall.

Amritsar

Sikh cultural & spiritual center, home to the Golden Temple complex & pilgrimage site.
